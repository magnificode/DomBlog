    • Process Process Process

  • Role / Importance process plays in an agency.
  • Three years working at Zenman, process has changed dramatically.
  • I've had a hand in that, along with Tomas and Tom
  • Give you insight so as to avoid early snags we ran into
  • Most important thing is to just start. Put something in place.
  • Preface with no process is ever perfect
  • One complaint I hear; bad time to implement new process
  • Zenman needed; a development process that would standardize practices. (each dev had their own class naming structure, methodologies, works fine when you're alone, but makes working in teams a nightmare)
  • Identify what you're trying to solve
  • Once identified you start thinking through how best to solve those isses, and what tools can assist you in doing so.

    • The Tools

    The tool doesn't dictate the process. It serves to amplify the effectiveness of the process.

  • Think of tools that will work in conjunction with your process.
  • Tools need to be interchangable. A better solution comes around, your process doesn't need to change for the tool.

    • Project Management

  • Projects and communications need a place to live.
  • Features; to-do lists, documents, file share, discussion.
  • Through iterations, and trial and error, we figured out what works for us, and our clients in BC
  • Through these iterations we now have pre-formatted to-do lists that walk through things like backing up a website, basic development needs, all the way up to testing protocols.

    • Agile Development

  • How many folks are agile trained, or at least know the principles of agile?
  • There are a huge amount of agile tools. Pivotal works well with our process. Gives us very granular view of each project and it's progress.
  • Most agile tools center around stories, and the acceptance of those stories by a Product Owner.
  • However many stories are accepted in a week, set a velocity that shows us how much we'll be able to complete the next week, until the project is complete.

    Global State

    Set data-state="something" on a slide and "something" will be added as a class to the document element when the slide is open. This lets you apply broader style changes, like switching the page background.

    State Events

    Additionally custom events can be triggered on a per slide basis by binding to the data-state name. 

    
Reveal.addEventListener( 'customevent', function() {
	console.log( '"customevent" has fired' );
} );
